Patron of Parish Priests

KEY TEACHINGS

THE PRIESTHOOD
St. John Vianney is the patron saint of parish priests. He spent 12-16 hours a day in the confessional, hearing thousands of confessions each year.

CONVERSION
He was a poor student who barely passed his exams. But God used him to bring thousands of souls back to the faith through his preaching and his holiness.

THE EUCHARIST AND CONFESSION
Vianney taught that the Eucharist and Confession are the two great medicines for the soul. He called confession "the tribunal of mercy."

FAMOUS QUOTES
"The priest is the love of the heart of Jesus."
"Confession is the soul's bath."
"Prayer is the oxygen of the soul."

WHAT JESUS WOULD SAY:
Jesus said, "I have come not to call the righteous, but sinners." St. John Vianney shows us that God's mercy is always available through the sacraments, especially Confession.

Source: Catechism of St. John Vianney; Lives of the Saints
