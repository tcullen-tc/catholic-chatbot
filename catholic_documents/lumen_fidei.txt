The Light of Faith

KEY TEACHINGS

FAITH AS LIGHT
Faith is not darkness but light. It illuminates all of human existence. Without faith, we walk in darkness; with faith, we see the meaning of life, suffering, and hope.

FAITH AND REASON
Faith and reason are not opposed. Faith opens the mind to truth. It does not replace reason but enables it to see further.

FAITH IN COMMUNITY
Faith is not private but communal. It is received in the Church and lived in relationship with others. Faith builds up the body of Christ.

WHAT JESUS WOULD SAY:
Jesus said, "I am the light of the world. Whoever follows me will not walk in darkness, but will have the light of life." Faith is not a burden but a gift that brings light to every aspect of life.
