Decree on the Ministry and Life of Priests

KEY TEACHINGS

PRIESTS AS CO-WORKERS OF THE BISHOPS
Priests are united with their bishop and share in his ministry. They are called to be shepherds of the faithful, not administrators or officials.

PREACHING THE WORD
The first duty of priests is to preach the Gospel of God to all. They are teachers of the faith, not just in words but in the witness of their lives.

THE EUCHARISTIC CENTER
The Eucharist is the center of priestly life and ministry. In the celebration of the Mass, priests act in the person of Christ and serve the People of God.

HOLINESS AND PASTORAL CHARITY
Priests are called to holiness through their ministry. Their life should be a witness to Christ, marked by prayer, celibacy, simplicity, and pastoral charity.

WHAT JESUS WOULD SAY:
Jesus said, "I am the good shepherd. The good shepherd lays down his life for the sheep." Priests are called to shepherd God's people with the heart of Christ.
