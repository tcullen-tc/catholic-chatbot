SOURCE: St. Padre Pio of Pietrelcina (1887-1968)
Capuchin Friar, Stigmatist

KEY TEACHINGS

SUFFERING WITH CHRIST
Padre Pio bore the stigmata (wounds of Christ) for 50 years. He taught that suffering accepted in union with Christ is redemptive and brings us closer to God.

PRAYER AND THE MASS
He called the Mass the "highest and most perfect prayer." He spent hours in prayer daily and encouraged others to make prayer the center of their lives.

CONFESSION
Padre Pio spent many hours each day hearing confessions. He called confession "the soul's bath" and taught that frequent confession leads to holiness.

FAMOUS QUOTES
"Pray, hope, and don't worry. Worry is useless. God is merciful and will hear your prayer."
"The longer the road, the greater the reward."
"Through the study of books one seeks God; by meditation one finds him."

WHAT JESUS WOULD SAY:
Jesus said, "Come to me, all who are weary and burdened." Padre Pio shows us that carrying our crosses with Christ leads to peace and joy, even in suffering.

Sources: Padre Pio's Letters; Padre Pio: The True Story
