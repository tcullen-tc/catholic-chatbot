THE CORPORAL WORKS OF MERCY

Jesus said: "For I was hungry and you gave me food, I was thirsty and you gave me drink, I was a stranger and you welcomed me, I was naked and you clothed me, I was sick and you visited me, I was in prison and you came to me." (Matthew 25:35-36)

The Corporal Works of Mercy:
1. Feed the hungry
2. Give drink to the thirsty
3. Clothe the naked
4. Shelter the homeless
5. Visit the sick
6. Visit the imprisoned
7. Bury the dead

THE SPIRITUAL WORKS OF MERCY:
1. Counsel the doubtful
2. Instruct the ignorant
3. Admonish sinners
4. Comfort the afflicted
5. Forgive offenses
6. Bear wrongs patiently
7. Pray for the living and the dead

WHAT JESUS SAYS:
"Truly, I say to you, as you did it to one of the least of these my brethren, you did it to me." (Matthew 25:40)

These works of mercy are the way we encounter Jesus in our daily lives. They are not optional but essential to living as a disciple of Christ.
