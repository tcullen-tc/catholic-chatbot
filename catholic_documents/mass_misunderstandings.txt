SOURCE: Common Misunderstandings About the Mass

MISUNDERSTANDING: The Mass is just a meal
- Truth: The Mass is both sacrifice and meal
- It is the re-presentation of Calvary
- It is the Wedding Feast of the Lamb

MISUNDERSTANDING: We are re-sacrificing Christ
- Truth: The Mass makes the one sacrifice of Christ present
- It is not a new sacrifice
- It applies the graces of Calvary to us

MISUNDERSTANDING: The Eucharist is just a symbol
- Truth: The Eucharist is truly the Body and Blood of Christ
- Transubstantiation changes the substance
- The appearance of bread and wine remains

MISUNDERSTANDING: Going to Mass is just a duty
- Truth: The Mass is the greatest gift
- It is our participation in heaven
- It is the source of grace

MISUNDERSTANDING: The priest is the main actor
- Truth: Christ is the main actor
- The priest acts in the person of Christ
- The people participate in Christ's offering

MISUNDERSTANDING: I don't need to go every Sunday
- Truth: Sunday Mass is a grave obligation
- It is the precept of the Church
- It is our participation in the Paschal Mystery

WHAT JESUS WOULD SAY:
Jesus said, "This is my body." His words are not symbols but reality. The Church faithfully teaches what Jesus taught.

Source: Catechism of the Catholic Church; Council of Trent
