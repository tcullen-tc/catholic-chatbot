SOURCE: Receiving Holy Communion

PREPARATION FOR COMMUNION
- State of Grace: Free from mortal sin; confess if needed
- Eucharistic Fast: No food or drink (except water) one hour before
- Proper Disposition: Reverence, attention, thanksgiving
- Spiritual Communion: When unable to receive, make an act of desire

HOW TO RECEIVE
On the tongue:
- Approach with hands folded
- Open mouth slightly, extend tongue
- The minister places the Host on the tongue
- Receive reverently, then return to your place

In the hand:
- Place one hand on top of the other, forming a throne
- The minister places the Host in your palm
- Step aside, consume the Host immediately
- Do not walk away with the Host

THE PRECIOUS BLOOD
- May be received from the chalice
- Intinction: Dipping the Host into the Precious Blood
- Only the Host is required for full Communion

AFTER COMMUNION
- Return to your place and kneel
- Spend time in silent prayer and thanksgiving
- St. Thomas Aquinas: "Nothing is more precious than this banquet"
- The grace of Communion lasts; stay recollected

SPIRITUAL COMMUNION
"I wish, Lord, to receive you with the purity, humility, and devotion with which your Mother received you... Come to me, Lord, for I long for you."

WHAT JESUS WOULD SAY:
Jesus said, "Whoever eats my flesh and drinks my blood has eternal life." Receive Communion with reverence, for you are receiving the King of kings.

Source: Catechism; General Instruction; St. Thomas Aquinas
