SOURCE: Our Lady of Lourdes (1858)

THE APPARITIONS
From February 11 to July 16, 1858, the Blessed Virgin Mary appeared 18 times to St. Bernadette Soubirous, a 14-year-old girl in Lourdes, France.

THE MESSAGE
Our Lady asked for:
- Prayer (especially the Rosary)
- Penance
- A chapel to be built
- Processions

THE MIRACLE OF THE SPRING
On February 25, Our Lady told Bernadette to "go drink at the spring and wash in it." Bernadette scratched in the muddy ground, and a spring appeared that now flows at 32,000 gallons per day. Many miraculous healings have been attributed to the waters of Lourdes.

THE APPARITION'S NAME
On March 25, the feast of the Annunciation, Bernadette asked the Lady her name. She replied, "I am the Immaculate Conception." This confirmed the dogma defined by Pope Pius IX four years earlier.

THE BASILICA
The Sanctuary of Our Lady of Lourdes is now one of the largest pilgrimage sites in the world, with millions visiting each year. The Church has officially recognized 69 miraculous healings.

WHAT JESUS WOULD SAY:
Mary said, "I am the Immaculate Conception." Her message at Lourdes calls us to conversion, prayer, and trust in God's mercy.

Source: St. Bernadette's Writings; Vatican Recognition
