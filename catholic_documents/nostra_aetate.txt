Declaration on the Relation of the Church to Non-Christian Religions

KEY TEACHINGS

A LANDMARK DOCUMENT
Nostra Aetate is one of the most significant documents of Vatican II. It opened a new era of dialogue between the Catholic Church and non-Christian religions.

THE JEWISH PEOPLE
The Church condemns all hatred and persecution of Jews. The death of Christ cannot be blamed on all Jews living then or today. God's covenant with the Jewish people remains.

MUSLIMS
The Church regards Muslims with esteem. They worship the one God, honor Abraham, and revere Jesus as a prophet. Christians and Muslims are called to work together for peace.

OTHER RELIGIONS
Other religions contain seeds of truth and goodness. The Church respects what is true and holy in them, while proclaiming Christ as the way, the truth, and the life.

WHAT JESUS WOULD SAY:
Jesus taught us to love our neighbors, including those of different faiths. We are called to respect the dignity of every person, who is made in the image of God.
