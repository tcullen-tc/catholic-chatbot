Dogmatic Constitution on the Church

KEY TEACHINGS

THE CHURCH AS MYSTERY
The Church, in Christ, is in the nature of a sacrament – a sign and instrument of communion with God and of unity among all people.

THE PEOPLE OF GOD
The chosen people of God is one: one Lord, one faith, one baptism. There is a universal call to holiness. All the faithful, whatever their condition or state, are called to the fullness of Christian life and to the perfection of charity.

THE MISSION OF THE LAITY
The laity, by their very vocation, seek the kingdom of God by engaging in temporal affairs and by ordering them according to the plan of God. It is their task to work for the sanctification of the world from within.

MARY, MOTHER OF THE CHURCH
Mary was given to the Church as a model and mother. In her, the Church admires and exalts the most excellent fruit of redemption and joyfully contemplates what she herself desires and hopes to be.

WHAT JESUS WOULD SAY:
Jesus founded his Church to continue his mission on earth. He said, "I am with you always, to the close of the age." The Church is the family of God, called to be holy as he is holy.
