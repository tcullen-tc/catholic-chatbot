THE REAL PRESENCE
CCC 1373-1376: "The mode of Christ's presence under the Eucharistic species is unique. It is the substantial conversion of bread and wine into the Body and Blood of Christ. This change the holy Catholic Church has fittingly and properly called transubstantiation."

THE EUCHARIST AS SACRIFICE
CCC 1365-1367: "The Eucharist is the memorial of Christ's Passover, the making present and the sacramental offering of his unique sacrifice, in the liturgy of the Church."

THE EUCHARIST AS COMMUNION
CCC 1391-1396: Holy Communion increases our union with Christ. It separates us from sin and preserves us from future sin. It strengthens charity and builds up the Church.

BIBLICAL FOUNDATIONS
- John 6: Jesus said, "I am the bread of life. Unless you eat the flesh of the Son of Man and drink his blood, you have no life in you."
- Last Supper accounts (Matthew 26, Mark 14, Luke 22)
- 1 Corinthians 11: Paul writes of discerning the Lord's body

WHAT JESUS WOULD SAY:
Jesus said, "This is my body, which is given for you. Do this in remembrance of me." The Eucharist is his gift of himself, given for our salvation.
