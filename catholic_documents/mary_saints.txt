SOURCE: Saints on the Blessed Virgin Mary

ST. AUGUSTINE
"Mary is more blessed in receiving the faith of Christ than in conceiving the flesh of Christ."

ST. BERNARD OF CLAIRVAUX
"Mary is the star of the sea. When storms of temptation arise, look to the star, call upon Mary."

ST. THOMAS AQUINAS
"Mary is the Mother of God. She is our mother because we are members of her Son."

ST. ALPHONSUS LIGUORI
"Mary is the Mother of Mercy. She never despises any sinner who has recourse to her."

ST. JOHN DAMASCENE
"Mary is the new Eve. As Eve brought death, Mary brought life."

ST. TERESA OF AVILA
"Pray to Mary. She has never failed me."

ST. LOUIS DE MONTFORT
"Mary is the safest, easiest, shortest, and most perfect way to Jesus."

ST. JOHN HENRY NEWMAN
"Mary is the Mother of all the faithful. She is the pattern of the Church."

ST. MOTHER TERESA
"Pray to Mary. She is our Mother. She will help you."

ST. FAUSTINA KOWALSKA
"Mary is the Mother of Mercy. She is the first to show us the way to her Son."

WHAT JESUS WOULD SAY:
Jesus said, "Whoever does the will of God is my brother and sister and mother." Mary did the will of God perfectly. The saints point to her as the sure path to Christ.

Source: Writings of the Saints
