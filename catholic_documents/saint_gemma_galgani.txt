SOURCE: St. Gemma Galgani (1878-1903)
Mystic and Sufferer

KEY TEACHINGS

SUFFERING WITH CHRIST
Gemma was a young Italian woman who received the stigmata (the wounds of Christ). She offered her suffering for the salvation of souls.

UNION WITH JESUS
She had visions of Jesus and her guardian angel. She was known for her deep prayer life and her love for the Eucharist.

TRUST IN SUFFERING
Despite terrible physical pain and spiritual trials, Gemma remained joyful and trusting. She said suffering was her greatest treasure.

FAMOUS QUOTES
"Suffering is my greatest treasure."
"Jesus, I ask for nothing but to love you."
"With Jesus, the cross is sweet."

WHAT JESUS WOULD SAY:
Jesus said, "If any man would come after me, let him deny himself and take up his cross and follow me." St. Gemma shows us that suffering, when united with Christ, becomes a source of grace and joy.

Source: St. Gemma's Autobiography; Letters
