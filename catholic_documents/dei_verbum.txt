Dogmatic Constitution on Divine Revelation

KEY TEACHINGS

GOD SPEAKS TO HIS PEOPLE
God, the Creator of all things, in his great and merciful goodness, chose to reveal himself to humanity. He speaks to us through Sacred Scripture and Sacred Tradition, which flow from the same divine source.

SCRIPTURE AND TRADITION
Sacred Tradition and Sacred Scripture form one sacred deposit of the word of God, committed to the Church. Both are to be accepted and venerated with equal sense of devotion and reverence.

INTERPRETATION OF SCRIPTURE
Since God speaks in Sacred Scripture through men in human fashion, the interpreter of Scripture must carefully investigate what meaning the sacred writer intended to express.

THE WORD OF GOD IN THE LIFE OF THE CHURCH
The Church has always venerated the divine Scriptures as she venerates the Lord's Body. She never ceases to offer to the faithful the bread of life, taken from the one table of God's Word and Christ's Body.

WHAT JESUS WOULD SAY:
Jesus said, "Man shall not live by bread alone, but by every word that proceeds from the mouth of God." The Scriptures are the living Word of God that guide us to eternal life.
