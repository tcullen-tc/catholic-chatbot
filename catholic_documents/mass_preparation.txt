SOURCE: How to Prepare for Mass

THE NIGHT BEFORE
- Review the Sunday readings
- Examine your conscience
- Plan to arrive early
- Prepare your intention for Mass

BEFORE MASS
- Arrive 10-15 minutes early
- Kneel and pray quietly
- Set aside distractions
- Unite your heart with the assembly

DURING MASS
- Follow the readings
- Sing the hymns
- Pray the prayers
- Enter into silence
- Receive Communion reverently

AFTER MASS
- Spend time in thanksgiving
- Make a spiritual communion if you did not receive
- Bring the graces into your week
- Live the Mass throughout the week

PREPARING CHILDREN FOR MASS
- Explain what is happening
- Use a children's missal
- Sit where they can see
- Be patient
- Model reverence

WHAT TO BRING
- A missal or worship aid
- Offering for the collection
- Your open heart
- Your intentions

FAMILY PREPARATION
- Read the Sunday readings together
- Discuss the Gospel
- Pray together before Mass
- Make Sunday a special day

WHAT JESUS WOULD SAY:
Jesus said, "Come to me, all you who are weary." Prepare your heart to meet the Lord in the Eucharist.
