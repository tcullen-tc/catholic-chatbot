Decree on the Apostolate of the Laity

KEY TEACHINGS

THE MISSION OF THE LAITY
The laity have their own role in the mission of the Church. By their very vocation, they seek the kingdom of God by engaging in temporal affairs and ordering them according to God's plan.

LEAVEN IN THE WORLD
The laity are called to be leaven in the world, transforming culture, politics, economics, and social life from within. They are not simply helpers of the clergy but have their own distinct mission.

EVERY CHRISTIAN IS AN APOSTLE
The apostolate of the laity flows from their union with Christ. Through baptism and confirmation, every Christian is called to be a witness to Christ in their family, workplace, and community.

THE FAMILY AS DOMESTIC CHURCH
The family is the first and vital cell of society. In the family, parents are the first teachers of the faith. The Christian family is called the "domestic church."

WHAT JESUS WOULD SAY:
Jesus said, "You are the salt of the earth... You are the light of the world." The laity are called to be salt and light in every corner of society, bringing Christ's love wherever they go.
