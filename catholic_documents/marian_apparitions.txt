SOURCE: Church-Approved Marian Apparitions

GUADALUPE, MEXICO (1531)
Our Lady appeared to St. Juan Diego, leaving her image on his tilma. She asked for a church to be built, showing herself as the mother of all peoples. The image remains miraculously preserved. Patroness of the Americas.

LOURDES, FRANCE (1858)
Our Lady appeared to St. Bernadette Soubirous, identifying herself as the Immaculate Conception. She revealed a spring with healing waters. Lourdes has become a place of pilgrimage for the sick and suffering.

FATIMA, PORTUGAL (1917)
Our Lady appeared to three shepherd children: Lucia, Francisco, and Jacinta. She asked for prayer, penance, and the daily Rosary. She revealed the "Fatima Prayer" and called for the consecration of Russia.

MIRACULOUS MEDAL (1830)
Our Lady appeared to St. Catherine Labouré in Paris. She asked for the Miraculous Medal to be struck, with the words: "O Mary, conceived without sin, pray for us who have recourse to thee."

LA SALETTE (1846)
Our Lady appeared to two children in France, weeping over the sins of the people and calling for conversion.

PONTMAIN (1871)
Our Lady appeared to four children in France during the Franco-Prussian War, protecting them from enemy soldiers.

WHAT JESUS WOULD SAY:
Jesus honored his mother and gave her to us. Mary's appearances remind us that she continues to intercede for us and lead us to her Son.
