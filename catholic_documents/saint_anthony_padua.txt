SOURCE: St. Anthony of Padua (1195-1231)
Doctor of the Church, Wonder-Worker

KEY TEACHINGS

THE GOSPEL PREACHER
Anthony was a Franciscan known for his powerful preaching. He brought thousands back to the faith. He is called the "Evangelical Doctor" for his teaching on Scripture.

FINDING WHAT IS LOST
Anthony is known as the patron saint of lost things. He taught that God can restore what is broken and find what is lost—including souls.

SIMPLICITY AND HOLINESS
Like St. Francis, Anthony lived in poverty and simplicity. He showed that holiness is not complicated but found in love of God and neighbor.

FAMOUS QUOTES
"Actions speak louder than words; let your words teach and your actions speak."
"The life of the body is the soul; the life of the soul is God."

WHAT JESUS WOULD SAY:
Jesus said, "Go into all the world and preach the Gospel." St. Anthony shows us that we are all called to share the Good News, whether through words or actions.
