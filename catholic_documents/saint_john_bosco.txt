SOURCE: St. John Bosco (1815-1888)
Founder of the Salesians

KEY TEACHINGS

EDUCATION WITH LOVE
Don Bosco educated street children in Turin, Italy. He taught with kindness rather than punishment. His method was "reason, religion, and kindness."

PREVENTIVE SYSTEM
He believed that education should prevent sin rather than punish it. He built schools, workshops, and oratories for young people.

MOTHER MARY
Bosco had a deep devotion to Mary, whom he called "Mary Help of Christians." He attributed all his work to her intercession.

FAMOUS QUOTES
"Run, jump, make noise, but do not sin."
"It is enough that you are young for me to love you."
"Do good, be cheerful, and let the birds sing."

WHAT JESUS WOULD SAY:
Jesus said, "Let the little children come to me." St. John Bosco shows us that every young person deserves love, education, and the chance to know Christ.

Source: Memoirs of the Oratory; Lives of St. John Bosco
