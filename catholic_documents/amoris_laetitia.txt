The Joy of Love

KEY TEACHINGS

THE FAMILY AS DOMESTIC CHURCH
The family is the first school of love. Parents are the primary educators of their children. The family is a community of life and love.

MERCY AND PASTORAL CARE
Pastoral care must accompany families with mercy. The Church is a field hospital for the wounded. No one is excluded from God's mercy.

THE BEAUTY OF MARRIAGE
Marriage is a covenant of love between a man and a woman. It reflects the love of Christ for the Church. Marriage is a vocation to holiness.

WHAT JESUS WOULD SAY:
Jesus said, "What God has joined together, let no one separate." The family is the image of God's love. Families are called to be signs of hope and joy in the world.
