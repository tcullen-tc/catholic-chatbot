SOURCE: St. Monica (332-387)
Mother of St. Augustine

KEY TEACHINGS

PERSISTENT PRAYER
Monica prayed for her son Augustine for 17 years before his conversion. She never gave up, even when he rejected her faith. Her tears and prayers finally brought him to Christ.

FAITHFULNESS IN SUFFERING
Monica endured a difficult marriage but remained faithful. She trusted God through years of suffering and eventually saw her husband convert shortly before his death.

A MOTHER'S HEART
Monica is the patron saint of mothers, wives, and difficult marriages. She shows us that prayer, patience, and persistent love can bring about miracles.

FAMOUS QUOTES
From St. Augustine: "My mother's tears were more powerful than my sins."
Monica's final words: "This one thing I ask: that you remember me at the altar of the Lord wherever you may be."

WHAT JESUS WOULD SAY:
Jesus taught us to "pray always and not lose heart." St. Monica shows us that persistent prayer, even when answers seem delayed, is never wasted. God hears every tear and every prayer.
