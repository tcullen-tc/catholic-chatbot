Martyrs of Africa

KEY TEACHINGS

COURAGE IN THE FACE OF DEATH
Twenty-two young men (Catholic and Anglican) were martyred by King Mwanga for refusing to renounce their faith. Many were between 14 and 25 years old.

ST. KIZITO (THE MOST FAMOUS)
Kizito was only 14 years old. He was the youngest of the group. He was baptized shortly before his death and went to his death singing and joyful.

ST. CHARLES LWANGA
Lwanga was the leader of the Catholic pages. He trained the young men in the faith and prepared them for martyrdom. He was burned to death for his faith.

WHAT THEY TEACH
These young saints show that faith can withstand any trial. They forgave their executioners and died with prayers on their lips.

FAMOUS QUOTES
"Kill us, but do not ask us to abandon our faith."
"You can burn our bodies, but you cannot destroy our souls."

WHAT JESUS WOULD SAY:
Jesus said, "Do not be afraid of those who kill the body but cannot kill the soul." The Uganda Martyrs show us that our faith is worth more than life itself.
