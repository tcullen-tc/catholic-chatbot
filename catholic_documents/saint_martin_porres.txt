SOURCE: St. Martin de Porres (1579-1639)
Patron of Social Justice

KEY TEACHINGS

HUMILITY
Martin was the illegitimate son of a Spanish nobleman and a freed slave. He became a Dominican lay brother and spent his life in humble service, sweeping floors and caring for the sick.

SERVICE TO ALL
Martin cared for everyone, regardless of race or status. He founded an orphanage and a hospital. He is known for his love for the poor and the sick.

UNIVERSAL BROTHERHOOD
Martin believed that all people are brothers and sisters in Christ. He worked across racial and social boundaries at a time when this was not common.

FAMOUS QUOTES
"Nothing is impossible with God. Nothing is too great to hope for from his bounty."
"Charity is the key that opens the door to the heart of God."
"Compassion is preferable to cleanliness."

WHAT JESUS WOULD SAY:
Jesus said, "I was sick and you visited me, I was in prison and you came to me." St. Martin de Porres shows us that serving the poor and suffering is serving Christ himself.
