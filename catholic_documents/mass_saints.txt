SOURCE: Saints on the Holy Mass

ST. JOHN VIANNEY
"If we really understood the Mass, we would die of joy."
"The Mass is the greatest act of love that can be given to God."

ST. THOMAS AQUINAS
"O precious and wonderful banquet, that brings salvation and contains all sweetness!"
"In the Mass, we are present at Calvary."

ST. PETER JULIAN EYMARD
"The Mass is the greatest work of God's love for us."
"The Eucharist is the center of the Church's life."

ST. TERESA OF AVILA
"After receiving Holy Communion, let us not lose any time. Let us go to thank our Lord."

ST. JOHN PAUL II
"The Mass is the sacrifice of Christ for the salvation of the world."
"The Eucharist is the source and summit of the Christian life."

ST. FAUSTINA KOWALSKA
"O living Host, my only strength, my one and only refuge!"
"At the moment of Communion, I feel as if I am being immersed in the ocean of God's mercy."

ST. MOTHER TERESA
"When you look at the crucifix, you understand how much Jesus loved you then. When you look at the Sacred Host, you understand how much Jesus loves you now."

ST. PADRE PIO
"The Mass is the most perfect prayer on earth."
"It would be easier for the world to survive without the sun than without the Mass."

WHAT JESUS WOULD SAY:
The saints who loved the Mass most deeply knew the treasure it contains. Their words invite us to enter more fully into the mystery.

Source: Writings of the Saints
