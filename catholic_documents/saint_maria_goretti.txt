SOURCE: St. Maria Goretti (1890-1902)
Youngest Canonized Saint

KEY TEACHINGS

FORGIVENESS BEYOND DEATH
Maria was 11 years old when she was attacked and stabbed multiple times for resisting sexual assault. Before she died, she forgave her attacker, Alessandro Serenelli.

HEROIC VIRTUE
Despite her young age, Maria chose death rather than sin. She became a model of purity and forgiveness for the whole Church.

THE POWER OF FORGIVENESS
Alessandro was convicted and imprisoned. After years, he had a dream of Maria, who appeared to him and forgave him. He converted, repented, and later attended her canonization.

FAMOUS QUOTES
"I forgive Alessandro, and I want him with me in heaven."
"God's forgiveness is greater than any sin."
"Purity is the flower of the soul."

HER CANONIZATION
St. Maria Goretti was canonized in 1950. Her mother attended the ceremony. Alessandro was also present, publicly repenting and praising God.

WHAT JESUS WOULD SAY:
Jesus said, "Forgive your brother from your heart." St. Maria Goretti shows us that forgiveness is not weakness but the greatest strength. She forgave the one who killed her, and her forgiveness saved his soul.
