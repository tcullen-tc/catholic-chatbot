SOURCE: St. Bernard of Clairvaux (1090-1153)
Doctor of the Church

KEY TEACHINGS

LOVE OF GOD
Bernard wrote extensively on the love of God. He described four degrees of love: love of self for self, love of God for self, love of God for God, and love of self for God.

HUMILITY
He taught that true knowledge comes from knowing oneself and knowing God. Humility is the foundation of all virtue.

MARY, STAR OF THE SEA
Bernard had a deep devotion to Mary. He called her the "Star of the Sea" who guides us to Christ.

FAMOUS QUOTES
"God is love, and he who abides in love abides in God."
"What we love we shall grow to resemble."
"Mary is the star that points us to Christ."

WHAT JESUS WOULD SAY:
Jesus said, "Love one another as I have loved you." St. Bernard teaches us that love is not just a feeling but the very essence of our union with God.

Source: On Loving God; Sermons on the Song of Songs
