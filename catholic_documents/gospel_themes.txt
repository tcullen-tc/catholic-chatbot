SOURCE: The Gospels - Themes

THE KINGDOM OF GOD
"The time is fulfilled, and the kingdom of God is at hand; repent, and believe in the gospel." (Mark 1:15)

FAITH
"Lord, I believe; help my unbelief!" (Mark 9:24)
"Ask, and it will be given you; seek, and you will find; knock, and it will be opened to you." (Matthew 7:7)

LOVE
"Love your enemies, do good to those who hate you, bless those who curse you, pray for those who abuse you." (Luke 6:27-28)
"By this all men will know that you are my disciples, if you have love for one another." (John 13:35)

FORGIVENESS
"Father, forgive them; for they know not what they do." (Luke 23:34)
"Forgive your brother from your heart." (Matthew 18:35)

MERCY
"Go and learn what this means, 'I desire mercy, and not sacrifice.' For I came not to call the righteous, but sinners." (Matthew 9:13)

SERVING OTHERS
"Whoever would be great among you must be your servant, and whoever would be first among you must be slave of all. For the Son of man also came not to be served but to serve, and to give his life as a ransom for many." (Mark 10:43-45)

PRAYER
"When you pray, go into your room and shut the door and pray to your Father who is in secret; and your Father who sees in secret will reward you." (Matthew 6:6)

HUMILITY
"Every one who exalts himself will be humbled, but he who humbles himself will be exalted." (Luke 18:14)
