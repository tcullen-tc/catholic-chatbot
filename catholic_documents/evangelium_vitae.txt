The Gospel of Life

KEY TEACHINGS

THE GOSPEL OF LIFE
The Gospel of life is at the heart of Jesus' message. Received by the Church, it is to be preached with the same courage as the Good News of the Gospel.

THE CULTURE OF DEATH
We are facing a new culture of death, where human life is no longer respected. Abortion, euthanasia, and the destruction of human embryos are crimes against life. No one can claim a right to kill.

THE INVIOLABILITY OF HUMAN LIFE
Human life is sacred from conception to natural death. Every person, regardless of condition, has a unique and irreplaceable dignity. The commandment "You shall not kill" has absolute value.

THE CULTURE OF LIFE
We are called to build a culture of life, where every person is welcomed, loved, and cared for. This is the path to true peace and justice.

WHAT JESUS WOULD SAY:
Jesus said, "I came that they may have life, and have it abundantly." Every life is precious. We are called to protect the most vulnerable among us.
