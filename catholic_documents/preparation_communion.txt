SOURCE: Catholic Teaching on Preparation for Holy Communion

STATE OF GRACE
CCC 1385-1389: To receive Holy Communion worthily, one must be in a state of grace (free from mortal sin). If conscious of grave sin, one must receive the sacrament of Penance first.

THE EUCHARISTIC FAST
One should fast from food and drink (except water and medicine) for one hour before receiving Communion.

PROPER DISPOSITION
- Reverence: Receive with respect, either on the tongue or in the hand as permitted
- Attention: Be attentive to what you are receiving
- Thanksgiving: Spend time in thanksgiving after Communion

SPIRITUAL COMMUNION
When unable to receive sacramental Communion, one can make an act of spiritual communion: "I wish, Lord, to receive you with the purity, humility, and devotion with which your Mother received you."

WHAT JESUS WOULD SAY:
Jesus said, "Take, eat; this is my body." He invites us to come to him, but also calls us to prepare our hearts to receive him worthily.
