Rich in Mercy

KEY TEACHINGS

GOD IS MERCY
Mercy is the greatest attribute of God. The Old Testament reveals God as "rich in mercy." In Jesus Christ, mercy is made visible and personal.

THE MERCIFUL FATHER
The parable of the Prodigal Son reveals the heart of the Father. God does not condemn but waits, forgives, and celebrates the return of his children.

MERCY AND JUSTICE
Mercy is not opposed to justice but fulfills it. Divine mercy brings justice to the sinner through forgiveness and restoration.

WHAT JESUS WOULD SAY:
Jesus said, "Be merciful, just as your Father is merciful." We are called to show mercy as we have received mercy. Mercy is the way of the Gospel.
