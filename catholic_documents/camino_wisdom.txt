SOURCE: Wisdom of the Camino

TRADITIONAL SAYINGS
- "The Camino provides." Trust that your needs will be met.
- "You don't choose the Camino; the Camino chooses you."
- "Walk the Camino for yourself; arrive for others."
- "Every step is a prayer."
- "The Camino begins when you leave; it ends when you return."

PILGRIM WISDOM
- "Pack light. You'll carry it far."
- "Your only competition is yesterday's you."
- "The mountain doesn't care who you are."
- "Leave your burdens at the Iron Cross."
- "Ultreia! Press on!"

SPIRITUAL LESSONS
- Letting go of expectations opens the heart
- The journey is the destination
- Community is found in walking together
- Hospitality is holiness
- The cross is carried one step at a time

WHAT THE CAMINO TEACHES
- Patience: the way unfolds step by step
- Trust: the path is provided
- Simplicity: you need less than you think
- Gratitude: a bed, a meal, a kind word
- Surrender: you cannot control the weather or the way

WHAT JESUS WOULD SAY:
The wisdom of the Camino echoes the Gospel. Jesus said, "Do not worry about tomorrow," and "Come to me, all who are weary." The Camino teaches us to live these words.
