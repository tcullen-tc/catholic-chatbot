SOURCE: St. Dominic Savio (1842-1857)
Patron of Youth and Students

KEY TEACHINGS

HOLINESS IN CHILDHOOD
Dominic was only 14 when he died. He was a student of St. John Bosco, who wrote his biography. He showed that young people can be saints.

DAILY DUTIES WITH LOVE
Dominic said, "I can't do big things, but I want everything to be for the glory of God." He did his schoolwork, prayers, and chores with great love.

COURAGE IN FAITH
He defended his faith against bullies. He gathered other boys to pray and encourage each other in holiness. He started a group called the "Company of the Immaculate Conception."

FAMOUS QUOTES
"Death rather than sin."
"I am not capable of doing great things, but I want everything to be for the glory of God."
"Let us go to heaven! I have never been so happy!"

HIS MOTTO
Dominic's motto was "Death rather than sin." He taught that even young people can resist temptation and live holy lives.

WHAT JESUS WOULD SAY:
Jesus said, "Let the little children come to me, and do not hinder them, for the kingdom of heaven belongs to such as these." St. Dominic Savio shows us that holiness is not about age but about love for God.

Source: St. John Bosco, Life of Dominic Savio
