Decree on the Missionary Activity of the Church

KEY TEACHINGS

THE CHURCH IS MISSIONARY BY NATURE
The pilgrim Church is missionary by her very nature. The mission of Christ continues in the Church, which is sent to all nations to proclaim the Gospel.

EVANGELIZATION OF ALL PEOPLES
All people have a right to hear the Good News. The Church is obliged to proclaim the Gospel to every creature, not out of superiority but out of love and obedience to Christ.

INCULTURATION
The Gospel must be proclaimed in a way that respects and enriches local cultures. The Church does not impose foreign customs but presents the faith in a way that speaks to each culture.

THE ROLE OF ALL THE FAITHFUL
Missionary work is not only for priests and religious. All the faithful share in the Church's missionary responsibility through prayer, witness, and support.

WHAT JESUS WOULD SAY:
Jesus commanded, "Go therefore and make disciples of all nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit." We are all called to share the joy of the Gospel.
