THE PILGRIM'S PRAYER
St. James, apostle and pilgrim,
guide us as we walk the way.
Help us to leave behind our burdens,
to open our hearts to grace,
and to find Christ in every encounter.
Pray for us as we journey,
that we may arrive at Santiago
with renewed faith and hope.
Amen.

BLESSING OF THE PILGRIM
May the Lord bless you and keep you
as you walk the way of St. James.
May his face shine upon you,
and may you find the light of Christ
in every step, in every encounter,
in every moment of weariness and joy.
May St. James guide your steps,
and may you arrive safely
at the house of the Apostle,
and at the house of the Lord.
Amen.

THE PILGRIM'S CREDO
I believe in the Way,
for Jesus is the Way.
I believe in the Truth,
for Jesus is the Truth.
I believe in the Life,
for Jesus is the Life.
I walk not alone,
for I am with Christ
and with all pilgrims
who have walked before.

ULTR EIA!
A traditional pilgrim greeting meaning "Go forward!"
Response: "Et suseia!" (And so onward!)

WHAT JESUS WOULD SAY:
Jesus said, "I am with you always, to the end of the age." On the Camino, as in life, we walk in his presence.

Source: Cathedral of Santiago; Pilgrim Traditions
