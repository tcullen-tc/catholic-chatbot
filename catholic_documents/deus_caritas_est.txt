God Is Love

KEY TEACHINGS

GOD IS LOVE
"God is love, and he who abides in love abides in God, and God abides in him" (1 John 4:16). These words from Scripture express the heart of the Christian faith. God's love is not a vague sentiment but a concrete reality.

EROS AND AGAPE
Love is a single reality, but with different dimensions. Eros (romantic love) needs to be purified to become agape (self-giving love). True love is not about taking but about giving. The love of God transforms our human love.

THE CHURCH'S CHARITABLE ACTIVITY
Love of neighbor is the practical expression of love of God. The Church's charitable activity is not a political or social program but a response to the immediate needs of the poor and suffering. It is an expression of faith.

WHAT JESUS WOULD SAY:
Jesus gave a new commandment: "Love one another as I have loved you." This love is not merely feeling but action. It is caring for the hungry, the thirsty, the stranger, the sick, the imprisoned.
