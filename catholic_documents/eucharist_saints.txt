SOURCE: Saints on the Eucharist

ST. THOMAS AQUINAS
"O precious and wonderful banquet, that brings salvation and contains all sweetness! Could anything be more delightful than this experience of God's goodness?"

ST. JOHN VIANNEY
"When we receive Holy Communion, we are as happy as the angels and saints in heaven. But we do not know it."

ST. TERESA OF AVILA
"After receiving Holy Communion, let us not lose any time. Let us go to thank our Lord, and we shall gain much more than by hours of prayer."

ST. PETER JULIAN EYMARD
"The Eucharist is the center of the Church's life. All the other sacraments are ordered toward it."

ST. FAUSTINA KOWALSKA
"O living Host, my only strength, my one and only refuge! I have nothing but You in heaven and on earth."

ST. MOTHER TERESA
"When you look at the crucifix, you understand how much Jesus loved you then. When you look at the Sacred Host, you understand how much Jesus loves you now."

WHAT JESUS WOULD SAY:
Jesus said, "I am with you always, to the close of the age." In the Eucharist, he keeps that promise, remaining with us as our food, our strength, and our hope.

Source: Lives of the Saints; Writings of the Saints
