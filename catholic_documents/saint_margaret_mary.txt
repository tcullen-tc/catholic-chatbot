SOURCE: St. Margaret Mary Alacoque (1647-1690)
Apostle of the Sacred Heart

KEY TEACHINGS

THE SACRED HEART OF JESUS
Jesus appeared to Margaret Mary, revealing his Sacred Heart burning with love for humanity. He asked for devotion to his Heart as a sign of his infinite love.

FIRST FRIDAYS
Jesus promised special graces to those who receive Communion on nine consecutive First Fridays. He asked for hours of adoration in reparation for sins.

TRUST AND MERCY
Margaret Mary faced opposition but remained faithful to her mission. She taught that the Heart of Jesus is a refuge of mercy for all who come to him.

FAMOUS QUOTES
"My Heart is so full of love for humanity that I can no longer contain it."
"Come to my Heart, and you will find rest."

WHAT JESUS WOULD SAY:
Jesus said, "Learn from me, for I am gentle and humble of heart." St. Margaret Mary shows us that the Heart of Jesus is the source of mercy, love, and peace for all who come to him.
