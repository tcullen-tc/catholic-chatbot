Confessions

KEY TEACHINGS

THE RESTLESS HEART
"You have made us for yourself, O Lord, and our hearts are restless until they rest in you." This famous line captures Augustine's journey from sin to grace.

CONVERSION AND GRACE
Augustine's conversion was not his own doing but a gift of God's grace. He discovered that God was pursuing him before he even sought God.

GOD'S MERCY
Despite his sinful past, Augustine found mercy. His life became a testament to the power of God's grace to transform even the greatest sinner.

FAMOUS QUOTES
"Late have I loved you, O Beauty ever ancient, ever new, late have I loved you!"
"Love, and do what you will."
"Our entire life is but a race after God."

WHAT JESUS WOULD SAY:
Jesus said, "I did not come to call the righteous, but sinners." St. Augustine's life shows us that no one is beyond the reach of God's mercy. Grace can transform any heart.
