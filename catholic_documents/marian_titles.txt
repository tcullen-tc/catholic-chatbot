SOURCE: Titles and Symbols of Mary

TITLES OF MARY

FROM SCRIPTURE
- Mother of God (Theotokos) - Luke 1:43
- Full of Grace (Kecharitomene) - Luke 1:28
- Blessed Among Women - Luke 1:42
- Woman (John 2:4; 19:26)
- Ark of the Covenant (typology from Revelation 11-12)

FROM TRADITION
- Our Lady of Guadalupe
- Our Lady of Lourdes
- Our Lady of Fatima
- Our Lady of Mount Carmel
- Our Lady of Sorrows
- Queen of Heaven
- Star of the Sea (Stella Maris)
- Refuge of Sinners
- Health of the Sick
- Comforter of the Afflicted

SYMBOLS OF MARY
- Lily: Purity
- Rose: Love and beauty
- Star: Guiding light (Star of the Sea)
- Moon: Reflection of the Son
- Ark of the Covenant: Carrying the Word of God
- Garden Enclosed: Her perpetual virginity
- Tower of Ivory: Her strength and beauty

WHAT JESUS WOULD SAY:
Jesus honored his mother, and the Church continues to honor her. Each title of Mary reveals a different aspect of her role in salvation history.

Source: Scripture; Litany of Loreto; Tradition
