On the Holy Spirit

KEY TEACHINGS

THE HOLY SPIRIT IS GOD
The Holy Spirit is not a creature but God. He is one with the Father and the Son in the Trinity. To deny the Spirit is to deny the fullness of God.

THE SPIRIT IN SCRIPTURE
The Spirit spoke through the prophets, descended upon Jesus at his baptism, and is poured out upon the Church. The Spirit is the source of holiness and unity.

WORSHIP OF THE SPIRIT
We glorify the Holy Spirit together with the Father and the Son. The Spirit is worthy of worship, not merely a created power or force.

WHAT JESUS WOULD SAY:
Jesus said, "When the Spirit of truth comes, he will guide you into all the truth." St. Basil teaches us to honor the Holy Spirit as the gift of God who makes us holy.
