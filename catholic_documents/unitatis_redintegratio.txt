Decree on Ecumenism

KEY TEACHINGS

THE CALL TO UNITY
Christ the Lord founded one Church. The division among Christians is a wound to the Church and a scandal to the world. All Christians are called to work for unity.

PRINCIPLES OF ECUMENISM
True ecumenism involves prayer, dialogue, and mutual understanding. It requires conversion of heart, renewal of the Church, and cooperation in serving humanity.

WHAT UNITES AND WHAT DIVIDES
Catholics must be faithful to the fullness of truth while seeking understanding with other Christians. Dialogue requires honesty, charity, and patience.

PRAYER FOR UNITY
Prayer is the soul of the ecumenical movement. We pray that all Christians may be one, as Christ prayed to the Father.

WHAT JESUS WOULD SAY:
Jesus prayed, "That they may all be one, as you, Father, are in me and I in you, that they also may be in us, so that the world may believe that you have sent me." Unity is Christ's gift and our task.
