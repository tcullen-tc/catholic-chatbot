BIBLE HISTORY TIMELINE

c. 2000-1500 BC: Patriarchal Period (Abraham, Isaac, Jacob)
c. 1250 BC: Exodus from Egypt
c. 1000 BC: King David establishes Jerusalem
586 BC: Fall of Jerusalem; Babylonian Exile
538 BC: Return from Exile
c. 250 BC: Septuagint (Greek translation of Hebrew Bible)
c. AD 50-100: New Testament writings
AD 382: Jerome begins Latin Vulgate translation
AD 1455: Gutenberg Bible printed
AD 1522: Luther's New Testament (German)
AD 1611: King James Version published
AD 1947: Dead Sea Scrolls discovered
AD 1965: Second Vatican Council promotes biblical studies
AD 1978: New International Version published
AD 2002: ESV (English Standard Version) published

Source: Museum of the Bible - Historical Timeline
