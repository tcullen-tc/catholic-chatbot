The Splendor of Truth

KEY TEACHINGS

THE TRUTH SHALL SET YOU FREE
"The truth will set you free" (John 8:32). Human freedom finds its authentic fulfillment in acceptance of the truth. Freedom is not license but the capacity to choose what is truly good.

THE MORAL LAW
There are moral norms that are universally binding. Not everything is relative. The Ten Commandments are not arbitrary rules but expressions of love: love of God and love of neighbor.

CONSCIENCE
Conscience is not a license to do whatever one feels. It is the judgment of reason whereby the human person recognizes the moral quality of a concrete act. Conscience must be formed in truth.

THE CALL TO HOLINESS
All are called to holiness. The moral life is not about following rules but about becoming the person God created us to be. Holiness is the fullness of Christian life.

WHAT JESUS WOULD SAY:
Jesus said, "If you love me, keep my commandments." The commandments are not burdens but a path to true freedom and happiness. They show us how to love God and neighbor.
