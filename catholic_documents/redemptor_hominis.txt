The Redeemer of Man

KEY TEACHINGS

CHRIST THE REDEEMER
Jesus Christ is the center of history and the universe. In him, the mystery of humanity is fully revealed. Every human person finds their true meaning in Christ.

THE DIGNITY OF THE HUMAN PERSON
Man is the primary and fundamental way of the Church. The Church's mission is to serve the dignity of every person, created in God's image and redeemed by Christ.

TRUTH AND FREEDOM
True freedom is found in following Christ. Freedom is not license but the ability to choose what is truly good. The Church defends human freedom by proclaiming the truth.

WHAT JESUS WOULD SAY:
Jesus said, "I am the way, and the truth, and the life." In him, we find the meaning of our existence. To follow Christ is to become fully human.
