On the Incarnation

KEY TEACHINGS

WHY GOD BECAME MAN
The Word of God became man so that we might become divine. He took our humanity to heal it, to free us from death, and to restore us to the image of God.

THE LOGIC OF THE INCARNATION
Only the Creator could recreate what was broken. Only the Author of life could conquer death. Only the Word who made us could remake us.

DEATH CONQUERED BY DEATH
Christ died not because he had to, but to destroy death. In the body, he destroyed the power of death; in the resurrection, he opened the way to eternal life.

WHAT JESUS WOULD SAY:
Jesus said, "I came that they may have life, and have it abundantly." St. Athanasius shows us that in Christ, God entered our humanity to raise us to share in his divine life.
