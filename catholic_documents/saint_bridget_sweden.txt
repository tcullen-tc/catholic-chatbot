SOURCE: St. Bridget of Sweden (1303-1373)
Foundress of the Bridgettines

KEY TEACHINGS

PASSION OF CHRIST
Bridget received visions of the Passion of Christ. Her "Revelations" describe the suffering of Jesus in vivid detail, calling Christians to meditate on his love.

PRAYER AND ACTION
Bridget was a wife and mother of eight children. After her husband's death, she founded a religious order. She showed that holiness is possible in every state of life.

CALL TO RENEWAL
She traveled to Rome and called for reform of the Church. She was a voice for renewal in a time of crisis.

FAMOUS QUOTES
"Lord, show me your way, and make me ready to walk in it."
"The more you honor me, the more I will bless you."

WHAT JESUS WOULD SAY:
Jesus said, "If anyone serves me, he must follow me." St. Bridget shows us that we can follow Christ whether married or religious, in action or in prayer.

Source: Revelations of St. Bridget; Lives of the Saints
