SOURCE: St. Blaise (c. 3rd-4th Century)
Martyr, Patron of Throat Ailments

KEY TEACHINGS

HEALING MINISTRY
Blaise was a bishop and physician. He healed the sick and was martyred for his faith. He is invoked especially for throat ailments.

BLESSING OF THROATS
The blessing of St. Blaise is given on his feast day (February 3) using crossed candles. It is a sign of trust in God's healing power.

FAITH IN SUFFERING
Blaise remained faithful even in persecution. He showed that physical suffering can be united with Christ's suffering.

FAMOUS QUOTES
"God is the healer of body and soul."
"Trust in God, who gives life to the dead and calls into existence the things that do not exist."

WHAT JESUS WOULD SAY:
Jesus said, "I am the way, and the truth, and the life." St. Blaise shows us that our health and healing are in God's hands, and we trust in his mercy.

Source: Acts of the Martyrs; Lives of the Saints
