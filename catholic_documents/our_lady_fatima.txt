SOURCE: Our Lady of Fatima (1917)

THE APPARITIONS
From May 13 to October 13, 1917, the Blessed Virgin Mary appeared six times to three shepherd children: Lucia dos Santos (age 10) and her cousins Francisco and Jacinta Marto (ages 9 and 7) in Fatima, Portugal.

THE MESSAGE
Our Lady asked for:
- Daily recitation of the Rosary
- Reparation for sins against the Immaculate Heart
- Consecration of Russia to her Immaculate Heart
- The Five First Saturdays devotion

THE THREE SECRETS
1. A vision of hell where sinners suffer
2. A warning that World War I would end, but another would begin if people did not stop offending God
3. A vision of the Pope walking through a city of the dead (interpreted as the 1981 assassination attempt on St. John Paul II)

THE MIRACLE OF THE SUN
On October 13, 1917, a crowd of 70,000 witnessed the sun dance in the sky, change colors, and seem to fall toward the earth. This miracle convinced many of the apparitions' authenticity.

THE FATIMA PRAYER
"O my Jesus, forgive us our sins, save us from the fires of hell, lead all souls to heaven, especially those in most need of thy mercy."

WHAT JESUS WOULD SAY:
Our Lady of Fatima calls us to prayer, penance, and conversion. Her message is urgent and timeless: return to God and pray the Rosary for peace.

Source: Sister Lucia's Memoirs; Vatican Recognition
