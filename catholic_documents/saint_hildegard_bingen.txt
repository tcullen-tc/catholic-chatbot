SOURCE: St. Hildegard of Bingen (1098-1179)
Doctor of the Church

KEY TEACHINGS

THE UNIVERSE AS GOD'S WORK
Hildegard was a mystic, composer, scientist, and theologian. She saw all creation as alive with the presence of God. She called this "viriditas" (greenness), the life-giving power of God.

PROPHETIC VOICE
She wrote to popes and emperors, calling them to repentance. She was not afraid to speak truth to power.

WOMAN OF WISDOM
Hildegard was the fourth woman named a Doctor of the Church. Her writings on creation, healing, and spirituality continue to inspire.

FAMOUS QUOTES
"The earth is at the same time mother, she is mother of all that is natural, mother of all that is human."
"I am a feather on the breath of God."

WHAT JESUS WOULD SAY:
Jesus said, "Consider the lilies of the field, how they grow." St. Hildegard shows us that all creation reveals God's love and calls us to reverence and care for the earth.

Source: Scivias; Letters of St. Hildegard
